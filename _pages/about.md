I am currently in the third year of my PhD under Prof. [Laurent Massoulié](https://www.msr-inria.fr/researchers/laurent-massoulie/). My research focuses on the spectrum of matrices associated to large sparse random graphs, and their applications to several variants of community detection. I am also interested in
various problems related to graph theory, from inference to graph embeddings.

I am also teaching assistant under [Ana Bušić](https://www.di.ens.fr/~busic/), on network models and algorithms. In my free time, I am a volunteer web developer for the [Klub Dev ENS](https://www.eleves.ens.fr/kde/about/)
